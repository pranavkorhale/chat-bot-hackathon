------------------------------
- Unified Mental Health Response Initiative
  Dispatches mental health professionals before police for non-violent mental health calls.

- Clinician Led Community Response (CLCR)
  Operates downtown from 10 AM to 8 PM daily with plainclothes crisis responders.
